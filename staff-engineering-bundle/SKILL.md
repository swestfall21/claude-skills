# Staff engineering bundle

Use this bundle for ambiguous, cross-team, and high-impact work that needs strategic direction.

## Activated skills

When this bundle is invoked, read each of the following skills' SKILL.md files and apply them for the rest of the session:

- staff-engineer-mode
- decision-making-guardrails
- stakeholder-communication
- launch-readiness-gate

## Operating posture

- reduce ambiguity into clear decisions
- create leverage for the organization
- align stakeholders around trade-offs and next steps
- keep the work launch-ready and sustainable
